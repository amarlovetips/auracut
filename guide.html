📖 Step-by-Step User Guide

1

Upload Video File

Drag and drop your video file into the designated upload area or click to select a video from your device. Supported formats include MP4, WebM, MOV, and AVI.

2

Configure Filters & Protection Settings

Customize the Anti-Copyright bypass filters (Micro-Tilt, Cinematic Vignette, Micro-Pulse Motion, Audio Phase Spatializer). Watermark and 6s Outro are active by default. Enter an Access License Key if you hold authorized credentials to unlock the toggle controls.

3

Start Hardware Processing & Export MP4

Click the Start Processing & Export MP4 button. AuraCut will render frames locally in real-time using GPU hardware acceleration. Monitor rendering progress in percentage.

4

Download Output Video

Once rendering reaches 100%, the browser will automatically initiate the download of your processed MP4 video file.

🛠️ How AuraCut Engine Works

AuraCut is a client-side video processing system operating inside the browser. It combines HTML5 Canvas 2D rendering, Web Audio graph synthesis, and WebCodecs H.264 MP4 hardware encoding to alter video and audio signatures without uploading media to external servers.

  • 100% Browser Local Processing: All video decoding and encoding happens on your local GPU/CPU.
  • 10 Anti-Copyright Bypass Engines: Includes Micro-Tilt (0.4° Angle), Cinematic Vignette, Micro-Pulse Breathing, Audio Phase Spatialization, 24 FPS Frame Skipping, and TV Static/Dirt particles.
  • Stealth Asset Protection: All system assets and verification keys are routed via internal server proxies to ensure stealth execution.
